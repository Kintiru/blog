# Long division

$$
\begin{alignat}{}
&\int a\frac{f(x)}{g(x)} dx \\
&\text{and} \\
&\text{degree of } f \geq \text{degree of } g
\end{alignat}
$$

# subsitution

$$
\begin{alignat}{}
&\int af'(x)f(x) dx \\
&\int a\frac{f'(x)}{f(x)} dx
\end{alignat}
$$
