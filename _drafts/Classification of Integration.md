# Long division

$$
\begin{align}{}
&\int a\frac{f(x)}{g(x)} dx \\
&\text{and} \\
&\text{degree of } f \geq \text{degree of } g
\end{align}
$$

# subsitution

$$
\begin{align}
&\int af'(x)[f(x)]^n \,dx \quad \text{when } n \neq 0
\end{align}

# Completing the squares

$$
\begin{align}
&\int \frac{a}{1+(x + b)^2} \,dx \\
&\int \frac{a}{\sqrt{1+(x+b)^2}} \,dx \\
\end{align}
$$
