# Coulomb's Law and Electric Fields

$$
|F_E| = \frac{kq_1q_2}{r^2} \\
k = 1/4 \pi \epsilon_0 = 9.0 \times 10^9 (N \cdot m^2)/C^2
$$

$$
\vec{E} = \frac{\vec{F}_E}{q} = \frac{kq}{r^2}
$$
